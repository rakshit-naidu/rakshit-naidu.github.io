Rakshit (Rocket) Naidu

I am a first-year PhD in Machine Learning (ML) student at ISyE, Georgia Tech. I am a recipient of the Thomas H. Johnson Fellowship award for the academic year of 2023-24.
I'm beyond fortunate to be co-advised by the awesome Professor Juba Ziani and Professor Kai Wang.
While my research interests hover around Responsible ML and Trustworthy AI, specifically tackling privacy and fairness issues in machine learning, I'm currently exploring how to register societal impact of AI applications through optimization.

Previously, I graduated with a Masters in Information Technology (MSIT-PE) degree from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). During my time at CMU, I worked as a Research Assistant under Professor Hoda Heidari during Spring-Summer 2023 and I was also a Visiting Research Scholar at Syracuse University under Professor Ferdinando Fioretto during Summer 2022.

If you have any queries related to my work or a potential collaboration, please feel free to reach out to me!

In my free time, I play badminton 🏸 and soccer ⚽. I also used to play Badminton for CMU (check out their Instagram page for more info: @cmubadminton!).
