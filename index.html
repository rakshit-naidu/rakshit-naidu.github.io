Rakshit (Rocket) Naidu

I am an incoming PhD in Computer Science and Engineering student at Georgia Tech. I am a recipient of the Stewart Topper Fellowship award for the academic year of 2023-24.
My research interests hover around Resposible ML and Trustworthy AI, specifically tackling privacy and fairness issues in machine learning, from an algorithmic perspective .

Previously, I graduated with a Masters in Information Technology (MSIT-PE) degree from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). During my time at CMU, I worked as a Research Assistant under Professor Hoda Heidari during Spring-Summer 2023 and I was also a Visiting Research Scholar at Syracuse University under Professor Ferdinando Fioretto during Summer 2022.

I'm incredibly passionate about mentoring aspiring, next-gen researchers in my field of interests (as it reminds me of the times I lacked guidance and resources on pursuing an academic career during my undergrad days)! Don't hesitate to reach out to me (I promise I won't bite :P)!
If you have any queries related to my work or a potential collaboration, please feel free to connect with me!

Update: I am actively seeking AI/ML research internship positions for Summer 2024 ! Please reach out if you have any leads!
